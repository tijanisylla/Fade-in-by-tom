# Fade By Tom - Family Hair Salon Website

## Project Overview
Landing page website for Fade By Tom, a family barbershop located in Chicago, IL.

## Business Information
- **Name:** Fade By Tom - Family Hair Salon
- **Address:** 5812 Irving Park Rd, Chicago, IL 60634
- **Phone:** 773-685-7744
- **Clientele:** Men, Women, Kids

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Single-page landing site
- Dark theme with gold/red/orange accents
- Google Maps embed
- Business hours display
- Contact information
- Service listings
- Photo gallery

## What's Been Implemented (Feb 2026)
- ✅ Hero section with logo, tagline, and CTA buttons
- ✅ Navigation with smooth scrolling
- ✅ About section with storefront image and stats
- ✅ Services section (7 services: Fades, Mohawk, Linings, Blow Out, Eyebrows, Razor Fades, Full Graphics)
- ✅ Gallery section with shop photos (5 images)
- ✅ Hours & Location section with Google Maps
- ✅ Contact information with clickable phone/address
- ✅ Footer with animated barber pole strip
- ✅ Mobile responsive design
- ✅ Custom color palette implementation
- ✅ Oswald + Manrope typography
- ✅ Fade-in animations on scroll

## Tech Stack
- Frontend: React
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Icons: Lucide React
- Fonts: Google Fonts (Oswald, Manrope)

## Color Palette
